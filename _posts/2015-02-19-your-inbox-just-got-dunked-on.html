Don't have time to visit Dribbble for the latest and greatest? Alleyooop allows for customized summaries of the week's best shots.

{{ page.date | date: '%B %d, %Y' }}
Published in {{ page.tag }}
{{ page.comments }}

I know, for a fact, I'm addicted to Dribbble— I check it before every project for inspiration and several times a day just to see what what is trending. For all intents and purposes, I do not need any other reminder of what is happening there. However, my friend and co-worker, Josh Beckman, does.

I sent that ungrateful bastard one of my Dribbble invites over a year ago an he has only posted one shot. His excuse(s)? "I just don't have time to get on there... I forget it exists... It's awful to look at on my small phone..."

To thrust Mr. Beckman back into the game, we came up with Alleyooop: a customizable newletter of Dribbble shots. You can adjust frequency of the newsletter and the type of shots you want to follow. Currently, we only show the most popular shots (views + likes) from either Animated or nonanimated shots, but hope to allow even more filtration, such as location and by tag.

I think the newsletter can be quite handy for not only Beckman, but other people too good or too busy (or too smart) to visit Dribbble regularly. Give it a try!
