Who I am

My name is Josh Martin and I am a former northwestern Pennsylvanian now based in Chicago working as Creative Director for Threadmeup.

Previously, Chicago Sun-Times, Goosebumps Book Club

What I Do

I work primarily in UI/UX and front-end development. I layout the architecture of websites and apps via sitemaps and wireframes, as well as create their interface through mockups, prototyping, and ultimately HTML/CSS/JS.

I also spend a great deal of time working in branding and illustration.

Experience

My experience, thus far, as been in working in e-commerce, data visualization, and large-scale digital news media. I have helped shape products all across the United States, as well as in the Caribbean and Ireland.

For more details on my past job experience and various professional proficiencies, please see my Linkedin.
