My name is Josh Martin and I am a Designer and Illustrator from Pittsburgh living in Chicago

Currently, making lives easier for artists and entrepreneurs at Threadmeup.

I went to school for animation, traditional print design, and creative writing. This greatly influences the style of work I do today which tends to be minimalistic and playful.

[At the moment] I am most passionate about web/app UI/UX (user-interface/user-interaction) and Illustration. I think the two fulfill my need to be creative and express myself, as well my insatiable need to tinker and solve problems.

With that being said, my interests don't start and end in those two areas. I love having my hands in a little bit of everything. I tend to pick up skills here-and-there for projects which has lead to learning web development, typography, branding, and copywriting.

My experience, thus far, as been in working in local e-commerce, data visualization, and large-scale digital news media. I have helped shape products all across the United States, as well as in the Caribbean and Ireland.

I identify most as a problem-solver. I aim to create clear, efficient, and most importantly, objective, solutions to product-oriented problems. I make sure said solutions are cohesive with branding and voice, as well as within development capabilities and any imposed limitations or constraints. I also feel comfortable in taking charge of projects and leading/teaching others.

Outside of client responsibilities, I manage a great deal of personal projects, such as Squawk, a movie review platform, and UXFairy, a resource UI/UX library.

If you're interested in connecting, you can find me at Music Box Theatre, watching wonderful midnight movies like Friday the 13th, at Logan Arcade sipping beer and playing Tron pinball, or at Bottom Lounge singing along with bands like Real Friends.
