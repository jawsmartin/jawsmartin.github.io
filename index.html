After completing a Bachelor of Applied Media Arts at the Edinboro University of Pennsylvania, I worked as a Front-End Developer for @power2switch, Lead UI/UX Engineer for @suntimes, and as Creative Director for @threadmeup.

Currently, I am an independent designer building with @werewolf3d, @Soapbox, among others. I may be available for your project, too.

Background

I tend to work primarily with development teams, creating wireframes, high-fidelity mockups, prototypes, UI kits, and brand assets/styleguides for web and mobile products. When needed, I also assist in front-end development: HTML5, CSS3/SASS, and Javascript.

I would describe my design style as functionality-over-aesthetic, boring, and developer-friendly. I love dry code, rigid brand guidelines, a well-kept framework.

In my spare time, I enjoy Pittsburgh sports, playing basketball, and painting. I also manage a variety of side projects.
